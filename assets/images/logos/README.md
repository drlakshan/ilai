# College and University Logos

This directory contains logo images for the colleges and universities displayed on the colleges page.

## Required Logo Files

To display logos on the colleges page, save individual logo images with the following filenames:

1. `ceylon-college-physicians.png` - Ceylon College of Physicians
2. `sri-lanka-college-paediatricians.png` - Sri Lanka College of Paediatricians
3. `physiological-society-sri-lanka.png` - The Physiological Society of Sri Lanka
4. `college-otorhinolaryngologists.png` - College of Otorhinolaryngologists and Head & Neck Surgeons of Sri Lanka
5. `university-kelaniya.png` - University of Kelaniya
6. `university-sri-jayewardenepura.png` - University of Sri Jayewardenepura
7. `sri-lanka-college-sexual-health.png` - Sri Lanka College of Sexual Health and HIV Medicine
8. `actssl.png` - Association of Cardiothoracic and Thoracic Surgeons of Sri Lanka
9. `university-colombo.png` - University of Colombo
10. `slcomm.png` - Sri Lanka College of Military Medicine
11. `nutritional-society-sri-lanka.png` - Nutritional Society of Sri Lanka
12. `comsaa.png` - Colombo Medical Faculty Alumni Association (CoMSAA)

## Image Requirements

- **Format**: PNG or JPG (PNG recommended for transparency)
- **Size**: Recommended 200x200px to 400x400px
- **Background**: Transparent or white background works best
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square or close to square for best display

## How to Extract Logos from the Slide

1. Open the slide image in an image editor (Photoshop, GIMP, or online tools)
2. Crop each logo individually
3. Save each logo with the corresponding filename listed above
4. Place all logo files in this directory (`assets/images/logos/`)

## Fallback

If a logo file is not found, the page will automatically display a FontAwesome icon instead, so the page will still look good even if some logos are missing.
