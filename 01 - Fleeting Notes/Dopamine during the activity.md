###### Dopamine during the activity
2022-11-04- 22:06





######  Note

Try to get a dopamine surge during the hard work not before or after the activity


CONTEXT:
its common to do an activity anticipating a reward - a dopamine surge at the end
however it may be counter productive
we should try to achieve a dopamine surge during the activity
