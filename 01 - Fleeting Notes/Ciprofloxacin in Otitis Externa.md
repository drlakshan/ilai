# Context:

**Topical Ciprofloxacin**:
- First-line for uncomplicated cases
- High local bioavailability with minimal systemic absorption
- Safe with perforated tympanic membrane
- Fewer side effects due to limited systemic exposure

**Oral Ciprofloxacin**:
- Reserved for severe otitis externa
- Essential for malignant otitis externa (MOE)
- High systemic bioavailability needed for invasive infections

**Resistance Concerns**:
- Growing issue with *P. aeruginosa* and *S. aureus*
- Culture sensitivity testing for non-responsive cases
- Consider antibiogram when selecting therapy
