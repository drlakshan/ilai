# What Did I learn?


1. Most common of benign neoplasms of the parotid gland (80% of benign parotid tumors per "Rule of 80s").

## Key Features
- Composed of epithelial, myoepithelial cells in varied stroma
- Incomplete fibrous pseudocapsule with microscopic extensions 
-[[Malingnat transformation risk of Plemorphic salivary adnoma]]

## Clinical Significance
- Simple enucleation contraindicated (45% recurrence rate)
- Time-dependent cancer risk makes active surveillance controversial
- Requires wide surgical excision with margin
