# Context:

Core management approach for uncomplicated otitis externa:

**First-Line Treatment**: Topical medications
- Antibiotics
- Corticosteroids
- Acetic acid drops

**Essential Components**:
- **Aural toilet**: Remove debris for medication penetration
- **Pain management**: Critical for patient comfort
- **Patient education**: Prevent recurrence

**When Systemic Antibiotics Indicated**:
- Severe cases with spreading infection
- Immunocompromised patients
