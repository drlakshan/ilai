# Syphilis - Sinonasal Manifestations

## Etiology
Treponema pallidum (spirochete)

## Sinonasal Involvement
Feature of **congenital** and **tertiary** disease

### Congenital Syphilis
Persistent rhinitis ("**snuffles**")

### Tertiary Syphilis
**Characteristic Lesion**: The gumma
- Localized granuloma
- Central necrosis
- Deep, destructive ulcer

**Destruction Pattern**:
- Destroys **both cartilage and bone** (vs TB)
- Septal perforation
- Palatal perforation
- **Severe saddle nose deformity**

## Diagnosis
**Serological Tests**: VDRL, RPR, FTA-ABS
**Histopathology**:
- Granulomatous inflammation
- Plasma cell infiltrate
- Endarteritis obliterans (pathognomonic)
