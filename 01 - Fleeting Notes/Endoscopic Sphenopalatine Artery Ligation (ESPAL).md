# What Did I learn?



### Surgical Landmarks and Procedure
- Endoscopic
- flap elevation posterior to MT in the lateral wall
- Crista Ethmoidalis 96% 
- branches are ligated or clipped

### Efficacy and Advantages
- **Success Rate**: Reported to be as high as 90-100%.
- **Advantages**: As a targeted endoscopic procedure, it has lower morbidity, shorter hospital stays, and higher efficacy compared to older techniques like internal maxillary artery ligation or external carotid ligation.
