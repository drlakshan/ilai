## Clinical Characteristics
- Benign vascular tumor
- Proliferative phase â†’ slow involution phase
- Highly vascular during proliferative phase

## Management Approach
- **First-line**: Oral propranolol (beta-blocker)
- Induces rapid involution
- Surgical excision avoided during proliferative phase (high bleeding risk, facial nerve injury risk)

## Clinical Decision-Making
- Medical therapy preferred over surgery
- Surgery reserved for failed medical management or specific complications
