###### Nasal dermoids
2022-09-20- 08:44





######  Note

CONTEXT:
 A patient was referred by Prof Sheriffdeen with a lump at the medial canthal region compressing on the Lacrimal Sac
 Possibility of nasal dermoid arose

key features

1. consist of mesoderm and ectoderm not all three layers as in teratoma
2. developmental error of fusion of anterior neuropore
3.  4- 45% possibility of intracranial connection
4. nasal pit may be present
5. complete excision after ruling out intracranial connection 
6. if present may need neurosurgical collaboration
