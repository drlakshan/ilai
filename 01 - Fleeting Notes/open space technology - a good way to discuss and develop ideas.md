first developed by Harrison Owen
by observation that best ideas came during coffee breaks

OST is distinct from a workshop:

1. **No Facilitator Control**: The group directs the content.
2. **Emergent Outcomes**: Solutions arise unpredictably from collective input.
3. **Scalability**: Works with 5 to 2,000+ people (used in corporations, NGOs, and even peace talks).
