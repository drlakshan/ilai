###### Wheel of Life
2022-08-19- 07:53





######  Note

CONTEXT: when describing how well we function at a given time there are many factors to consider. Here is a model proposed by Paul J Myer in 1960s. this was proposed to help people realise their goals.

Its a ten segment chart
1. Spirituality
2. Money and Finances
3. Career and work
4. Health and Fitness
5. Fun and recreation
6. Environment (home and work)
7. Community
8. Family and Friends
9. Partner and Love
10. Personal Growth and Learning
