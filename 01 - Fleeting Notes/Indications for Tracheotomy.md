# What Did I learn?

1. Prolonged Mechanical Ventilation
	1. Respiratory Distress
	2. Neuromuscular Diseases
	3. Depressed mental status (airway protection failure)
	
2. Pulmonary Toilet (secretion management)

3. Surgical Access
	1. complex head and neck surgeries
	2. OMF surgeries like facial fractures
	
4. Airway obstruction (relief of)
	1. Craniofacial abnormalities
	2. Tumors
	3. B/L vocal cord palsy
	4. angiooedema
	5. Ludwigs' Angina
	6. Epiglottitis/Supraglottitis
	7. blunt and penetrating head and neck trauma
