# AIMED: Core Competencies in AI for Medical Education

1. **Analytical and Data Mastery**: Combining data literacy with critical evaluation of AI tools, focusing on data understanding, interpretation, and critical assessment of AI technologies.

2. **Integration of AI in Clinical Practice**: Merging basic AI concepts, clinical application of AI, and patient-centered care, emphasizing practical use of AI tools in enhancing patient outcomes and centering on patient needs.

3. **Multidisciplinary Communication and Collaboration**: Integrating interdisciplinary collaboration with communication skills, focusing on effective teamwork among diverse professionals and clear communication of AI concepts.

4. **Ethical Leadership in Change Management**: Combining ethical and legal considerations with change management and leadership, stressing the importance of ethical awareness and leadership in adapting to AI advancements.

5. **Dynamic Learning and Adaptability**: A synthesis of continual learning and adaptability, highlighting the importance of staying current with AI developments and being adaptable to new technologies.
