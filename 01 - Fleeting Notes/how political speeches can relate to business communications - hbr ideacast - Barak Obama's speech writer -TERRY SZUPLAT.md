
What We Can Learn from Great Political Speakers - Key Ideas

  

1. Public speaking is a crucial skill for business leaders to master.
2. Public speaking skills can be developed through practice and training, not just natural talent.
3. Focusing on your unique perspective and story can help reduce anxiety about public speaking.Â 
4. Great speakers have thought deeply about their values, goals, and what they're trying to achieve.
5. Spending more time planning and preparing is key to effective public speaking, not just the writing.
6. The ending of a speech or presentation should focus on reinforcing the emotional connection and leaving the audience with hope.
7. Using simple, conversational language is crucial, even when speaking to intelligent audiences.
