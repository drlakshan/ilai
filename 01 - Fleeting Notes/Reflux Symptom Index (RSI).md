 Nine symptoms on a scale from 0 (noproblem) to 5 (a severe problem):

   1. Hoarseness or a problem with your voice
   2. Clearing your throat
   3. Excess throat mucous or postnasal drip
   4. Difficulty swallowing food, liquids, or pills
   5. Coughing after you have eaten or after lying down
   6. Breathing difficulties or choking episodes
   7. Troublesome or annoying cough
   8. Sensation of something sticking in your throat or a lump in your
     throat 
     Heartburn, chest pain, indigestion, or stomach acid coming up

  A total score greater than 10 may indicate a problem with LPR.
