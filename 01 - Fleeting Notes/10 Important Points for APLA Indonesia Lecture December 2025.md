# 10 Important Points for APLA Indonesia Lecture December 2025

**Conference**: Asia Pacific Laryngology Association Meeting
**Date**: December 5, 2025
**Location**: Indonesia
**Topic**: "AI in Laryngology: Your Next Essential Instrument"
**Speaker**: Dr. MTD Lakshan

## Core Message
AI is an augmentation tool, not a replacement - the laryngologist who embraces AI will define the future of the specialty.

---

## 10 Most Relevant Points

### 1. AI as Diagnostic Augmentation in Laryngeal Imaging
- **Point**: Deep learning CNNs detect and classify benign vs malignant laryngeal lesions with 98% accuracy
- **Clinical Impact**: Provides second pair of sharp eyes for early detection
- **Your Experience**: 25+ years ENT expertise enhanced by AI-powered image analysis
- **Relevance**: Real-time frame selection from stroboscopy improves diagnostic consistency
- **Example**: AI-highlighted suspicious lesions in laryngoscopy images catch subtle signs earlier

### 2. Voice as Diagnostic Biomarker
- **Point**: AI analyzes acoustic parameters to identify unique vocal fingerprints for screening
- **Clinical Impact**: Early detection of nodules, paralysis, neurological conditions (Parkinson's)
- **Your Experience**: Moving from subjective GRBAS assessment to objective, quantifiable data
- **Relevance**: Scalable screening tool for voice disorders before clinical symptoms
- **Example**: Voice biomarkers detect subtle changes indicative of pathology

### 3. Practical Research Workflow with NotebookLM
- **Point**: AI-powered tools synthesize expert-level knowledge from multiple sources in minutes
- **Clinical Impact**: Rapid evidence-based decision support for complex cases
- **Your Experience**: Daily workflow integrates NotebookLM for clinical research and lecture preparation
- **Relevance**: Live demo showing unilateral vocal fold paralysis management synthesis
- **Example**: Generate comparison tables, patient explanation scripts from recent literature

### 4. AI in Medical Education Transformation
- **Point**: AI enables personalized learning paths and virtual patient simulations
- **Clinical Impact**: Enhanced resident and medical student training with AI-assisted scenarios
- **Your Experience**: Senior Lecturer developing "Learning and Practicing Medicine When AI is in the Equation" course
- **Relevance**: Published work on pros/cons of AI integration in medical education
- **Example**: AI-powered flashcard systems, clinical case studies, rehearsal of difficult conversations

### 5. Agentic AI and Workflow Automation
- **Point**: Multi-agent systems create "living software" that adapts and collaborates
- **Clinical Impact**: Automated clinical workflows free physicians for patient-centered care
- **Your Experience**: Developed RAG chatbots, implemented LangChain/LangGraph/CrewAI systems
- **Relevance**: Move from static protocols to dynamic, context-aware clinical decision support
- **Example**: Dhamma ChatBot with document grading, response validation, hallucination detection

### 6. Ethical Framework for AI Adoption
- **Point**: Address black box problem, data privacy, and accountability in AI-assisted diagnosis
- **Clinical Impact**: Build trust through transparency and responsible AI implementation
- **Your Experience**: Buddhist principles of mindfulness and non-harm applied to AI ethics
- **Relevance**: Representational bias, systemic bias, allocative harm awareness critical for medical AI
- **Example**: Who is responsible when AI-assisted diagnosis is wrong? Clear accountability needed

### 7. Prompt Engineering as Clinical Skill
- **Point**: Effective AI interaction requires mastery of frameworks (COSTAR, CAPITAL, 4D)
- **Clinical Impact**: 20-80% productivity improvement with skilled AI usage
- **Your Experience**: Completed multiple Coursera courses (Vanderbilt, UC Davis, DeepLearning.AI)
- **Relevance**: "AI gives perspectives not answers" - critical thinking remains paramount
- **Example**: Question Refining Pattern, Chain-of-Thought prompting for complex clinical scenarios

### 8. Integration of AI Tools into Daily ENT Practice
- **Point**: Specific AI stack for ENT clinicians: ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, NotebookLM, Cursor
- **Clinical Impact**: Seamless workflow from literature review to patient communication
- **Your Experience**: Arogya Practice Software integrates Google Workspace with AI capabilities
- **Relevance**: Each tool serves specific purpose - large context analysis, code generation, audio summaries
- **Example**: Gemini CLI for comprehensive vault analysis, Claude Code for automation

### 9. Future Horizon: Surgical Robotics and Predictive Analytics
- **Point**: AI-guided precision in TORS, AR-assisted surgery, hyper-personalized voice therapy
- **Clinical Impact**: Real-time surgical guidance, complication prediction, adaptive rehabilitation
- **Your Experience**: Director of Tertiary Care Private Hospital positioning for AI-enhanced surgical workflows
- **Relevance**: Predictive models identify high-risk patients before complications occur
- **Example**: Voice therapy apps adapting in real-time to patient performance data

### 10. Start Small Strategy: One Tool, One Task
- **Point**: Begin AI journey with single tool (NotebookLM) for single task (literature synthesis)
- **Clinical Impact**: Reduces overwhelm, builds confidence, demonstrates immediate value
- **Your Experience**: 19-month AI journey from March 2023 ChatGPT exploration to advanced agentic systems
- **Relevance**: Collaborative future requires practical experimentation, not theoretical perfection
- **Example**: "Pick one tool, one task, and see how it enhances your practice" - actionable takeaway
