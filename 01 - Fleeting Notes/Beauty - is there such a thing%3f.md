# Begger who showed everything

Close to elite

elderly female begger

showed me the reality

of life and beauty


Oh what have been going after?

is it really worth it?

is this what we really want?

is not there any other pleasure?


Lord Buddha showed us the way

It is up to us to take the path

Delivering us from this suffering

Lets tred on THE path carefully and peacefully
















context:

There was a elderly begger showing me everything.
