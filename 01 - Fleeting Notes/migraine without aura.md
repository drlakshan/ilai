# Migraine with aura definition  
(31/12/2022, 15:00:44)

“Migraine without aura A. At least 5 attacks with the following characteristics: 1. Headaches lasting 4 - 72 hours with at least 2 of following: a. Unilateral site b. Pulsating quality c. Moderate to severe intensity d. Aggravated by physical activity 2. During headache at least one of following: a. Nausea or vomiting b. Photophobia and phonophobia 3. No evidence of related organic disease” ([“Vestibular migraine handout given by the american academy”, p. 1](zotero://select/library/items/5RWNZT68)) ([pdf](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/4FZREZG8?page=1&annotation=MJZDG53E))
