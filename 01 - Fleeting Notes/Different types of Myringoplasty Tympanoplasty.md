#otology #endoscopic-ear 

Type I : Involves the restoration of the perforated eardrum by grafting. 

Type II : Tympanic membrane perforations with erosion of the malleus. 
Grafting onto the incus or the remains of the malleus. 

Type III  Indicated for destruction of two ossicles, with the stapes still intact and mobile.
Graft placed onto the stapes. 

Type IV For destruction of all or part of the stapes arch.
Involves placing a graft onto or around a mobile stapes footplate. 

Type V 
If the footplate of the stapes is fixed.
