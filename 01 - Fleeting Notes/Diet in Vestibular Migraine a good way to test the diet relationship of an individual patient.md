# Note

1. give the dietsheet
2.  do a two week test of strict avoidance from trigger list and abundance from safe list
3. do symptoms improve?
4. confirm triggers
5. then do the confirmation test
6. slowly add foods back into diet every two days one at a time
7. start with less risky bottm of the list
8. eat plenty of that particular food
9. asssess for VM symtoms
10. keep or avoid



# Context:

there should be a way to decide which foods will be useful and which one to avoid
