# Note




Total Medial Maxillectomy : Complete removal of the lateral nasal wall including nasolacrimal duct and head of the inferior turbinate

Modified medial maxillectomy: A large middle meatal antrostomy from orbit to the nasal floor [[What is a mega antrostomy?]] However NLD and IT heads are preserved




# Context:


Operating on possible inverted papilloma patient who may require medial maxillectomy. I used this approach previously used in a patient with angiofibroma
