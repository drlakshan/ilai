# What Did I learn?


1. Ruthlessly do less
2. limit daily todo list to 5 and do the MIT (Most Important Task)
3. To DoNOT list of time wasters
4. Make "NO" your default response 
5. Identify and Fiercely protect "Golden Hours"
6. Eat the Frog at the Golden Hour
7. Time Box: set the start and end times
8. Think Less for repetitive tasks like choosing attires and conserve mental energy
9. 2 minute rule to eliminate mundane
10. avoid multitasking
11. Batch process
12. Limit your choices
13. Biggest motivator is making progress in meaningful work so track it diligently
14. Plan priorities on Monday and achievements on Friday
15. take strategic breaks
16. Repetition : biggest predictor of long term habits
17. consistent small actions compound unlike intensity leading to burnout
