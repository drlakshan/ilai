###### devise a plan to solve the problem - polyas method
2022-08-12- 07:29





######  Note

CONTEXT:

1. Have you seen it before? may be even in a slightly different form
2. Do you know a related problem? Do you know a theorem that could be useful?
3. Look at the unknown and think of a familiar problem
4. Here is a problem related to yours and solved before. Could you use it? Could you use the result? Could you use the method? Should you introduce an auxillary element in order to make its use possible?
5. Could you restate the problem? may be differently? Go back to the definitions.
6. Try to solve a related problem
7. Solve a part of a problem?
8. drop a part of the condition and see how far you can go
9. did you use all the data?
10. did you use the whole condition?
11. Have you used all the essential notions into account
