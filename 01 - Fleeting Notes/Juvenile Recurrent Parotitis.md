# Juvenile Recurrent Parotitis - Sialendoscopy Role

Self-limiting inflammatory condition in children with repeated parotid swelling episodes.

## Clinical Features
- Painful parotid gland swelling episodes
- Typically resolves around puberty
- Recurrent nature causes parental concern

## Sialendoscopy Applications
- **Diagnostic**: Visualize ductal abnormalities
- **Therapeutic**: Flush debris from ducts
- **Treatment**: Corticosteroid irrigation
- **Outcome**: Reduces frequency and severity of episodes

## Clinical Significance
- Minimally invasive intervention
- Both diagnostic and therapeutic utility
