
Dear Sir

Every once in a while,
a great surgeon appears. 
In addition to highly skilled hands, 
would own a kind heart and a caring soul.


It was you Sir, for me.
the manner you treated me 
gave me an example
how an ideal surgeon should be.

Thank you from the bottom of my heart
for all you have done. 
Not only for me,
but for the entire humanity. 

Only way I see fit
to repay the debt
would be to serve others to best of my ability 
for which I have made a determination
All thanks to you!

Wish you best of health and prosperity
for many many years to come !

Lakshan
