###### POTS
2022-11-21- 08:18

1. upright position causes inappropriate rise in heart rate
2. increased noraderanaline in the synaptic cleft
3. sometimes familial noradrenaline receptor or nitric oxide synthase enzyme abnormalities
4. may need betablockers
5. much common in females than males
