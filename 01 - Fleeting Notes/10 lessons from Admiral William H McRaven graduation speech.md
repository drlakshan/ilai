
1. **Start your day with a task completed**  

   - Making your bed each morning fosters discipline and creates a sense of accomplishment.  

  

2. **Find someone to help you paddle**  

   - Success requires teamwork, collaboration, and the support of others to overcome challenges.  

  

3. **Measure people by the size of their heart, not their physical attributes**  

   - True strength lies in determination, resilience, and character, not external appearances.  

  

4. **Get over being a "sugar cookie"**  

   - Accept that life is unfair, and keep pushing forward despite difficulties and setbacks.  

  

5. **Don't fear failure; embrace it**  

   - Failure builds resilience, character, and the ability to learn and grow stronger.  

  

6. **Take risks and dare greatly**  

   - Courage to take calculated risks can lead to extraordinary outcomes and personal growth.  

  

7. **Face down the sharks**  

   - Confront fears and challenges head-on to overcome adversity and build courage.  

  

8. **Be your best in the darkest moments**  

   - Remain composed and steadfast during chaos, as true strength reveals itself in adversity.  

  

9. **Sing when you're up to your neck in mud**  

   - Foster hope and optimism, even in the direst circumstances, to inspire yourself and others.  

  

10. **Never, ever quit**  

   - Perseverance and determination through hardship are essential to achieving meaningful impact.
