# Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) Principles

## 1. Cognitive Restructuring
- Challenging negative thought patterns
- Replacing them with balanced and realistic thoughts

## 2. Behavioral Activation
- Engaging in positive activities
- Improving mood and reducing avoidance behaviors

## 3. Exposure Therapy
- Gradually exposing to feared situations
- Building confidence and reducing anxiety over time
