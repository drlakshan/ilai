### PESTLE Framework

The PESTLE framework is a strategic management tool used to identify, analyze, organize, and monitor key external factors that can have an impact on an organization. By understanding these macro-environmental influences, businesses can make more informed decisions and anticipate future challenges and opportunities.

The acronym PESTLE stands for:

*   **P**olitical
*   **E**conomic
*   **S**ocial
*   **T**echnological
*   **L**egal
*   **E**nvironmental

### How is it Used?

Organizations use the PESTLE framework for various purposes, including:

*   **Strategic Planning:** To understand the business environment when developing long-term strategies.
*   **Market Analysis:** To assess the attractiveness of a potential new market or industry.
*   **Risk Management:** To identify potential external threats and prepare for them.
*   **Business Development:** To spot new opportunities for products, services, or expansion.
