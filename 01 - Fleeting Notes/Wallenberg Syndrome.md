# Note

1. Hoarseness : nucleus ambigus of Vagus and Glossopharyngeus
2. Dysphagia : nucleus ambigus of Vagus and Glossopharyngeus
3. Ipsilateral Facial Paresethesia : descending tract of trigeminal
4. Ipsilateral Horner's Syndrome : descending sympathetic tract
5. Contralateral pain and numbness of the upper and lower legs : spinothalamic tract
6. Ataxia : spinocerebellar tracts
