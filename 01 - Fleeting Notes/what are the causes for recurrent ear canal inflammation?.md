# Context:

Persistent or recurrent external ear inflammation - investigate underlying causes.

**Common Underlying Conditions**:
- Eczema
- Psoriasis
- Contact allergies (hearing aids, earrings, topical medications)

**Management Approach**:
- Identify and treat root cause (not just infection)
- Dermatology referral when indicated
- Address precipitating factors:
  - Excessive moisture exposure
  - Aggressive ear cleaning
  - Allergic contact dermatitis

**Patient Education**:
- Avoid ear canal manipulation
- Keep ears dry
- Identify allergen triggers

**Key Principle**: Recurrence signals underlying pathology - address causation, not just acute episodes.
