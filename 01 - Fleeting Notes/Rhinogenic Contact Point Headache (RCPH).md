# What Did I learn?

RCPH or Rhinogenic Contact Point Headache is a form of headache caused by an anatomical variation inside the nose such as a deviated nasal septum or spur making contact with a turbinate or nasal wall . 

diagnosis : local anaesthesia spray will abort the headache

treatment surgery septoplasty or turbinate reduction
