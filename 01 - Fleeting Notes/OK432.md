## References



1. OK-432, also known as Picibanil, is a lyophilized preparation of a low-virulence strain of Streptococcus pyogenes. It is used in medicine as an immunotherapeutic agent. 1 It functions as a biological response modifier and is approved in Japan as an anticancer agent. 2
Key applications of OK-432 include:
	•	Sclerotherapy: It is an effective treatment for lymphatic malformations (lymphangiomas), particularly macrocystic types in children. 3 	•	Cancer Immunotherapy: OK-432 is used to activate the immune system against cancer cells and is used in treatments for malignant pleural effusions and other cancers. 4 	•	Pleurodesis: The agent is used to treat conditions like prolonged air leaks after lung surgery by causing the pleural layers to adhere. 10
