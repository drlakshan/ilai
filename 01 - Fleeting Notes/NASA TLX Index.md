National Aeronautics and Space Administration Task Load Index
Used to measure the workload experienced by individuals while performing a task
Six dimensions
1. Mental Demand
2. Physical Demand
3. Temporal Demand
4. Performance
5. Effort
6. Frustation

Scale of 0-100
