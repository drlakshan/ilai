# What Did I learn?


Most powerful dynamic predictors of medullary thyroid carcinoma survival.

#### Key Markers
• Calcitonin doubling time: Primary prognostic indicator
• CEA doubling time: Secondary survival predictor

#### Prognostic Thresholds
• <6-12 months doubling: Poor prognosis indicator
• <2 years calcitonin doubling: Poor short-term survival
• Stable/slowly rising: Indolent disease course

#### Baseline Significance
• Preoperative calcitonin >300 pg/mL: High tumor burden/metastases
• Undetectable postoperative calcitonin: Likely biochemical cure

#### Clinical Application
• Serial monitoring post-treatment
• Guide surveillance intensity
• Treatment response assessment
