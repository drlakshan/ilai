Hamu wee atha Budu Piya
maga yayuthu pahadiliy
labena sanasuma athdutuwai
noya emaga ai balanne


sitha ridumani viyakee yana
daham osuwa adama bonna















context:

solution to the life's hurdles is Dhamma 
Nothing else
Disobedient unaffectionate kids 
Sick kids
Difficult life situations
Dhamma will help
