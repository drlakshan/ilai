# What Did I learn?

Pathologic TNM staging integrating tumor, node, metastasis factors for prognosis.

#### TNM Components
• **T (Tumor)**: Size and local extension
• **N (Nodes)**: Regional lymph node involvement
• **M (Metastasis)**: Distant spread presence

#### Age-Dependent Significance
• **<55 years**: Stage I-II only (excellent prognosis regardless nodal status)
• **≥55 years**: Higher stages correlate directly with worse survival

#### ATA Risk Stratification Integration
• Low risk: Excellent prognosis
• Intermediate risk: 5-15% recurrence
• High risk: >15% recurrence, mortality risk

#### Adverse Nodal Features
• >5 positive lymph nodes
• Metastases ≥3 cm
• Extranodal extension present
