What are the methods to reduce bleeding suring FESS?::
	1. Elevation of the head end of the bed to 30 degrees ( not too high reducing the cerebral blood flow not too low no reduction in bleeding)
	2. TIVA ( Ramifentanyl and Propofol e.g)
	3. Local Epinephrine
	4. Lignocaine with Adrenaline
	5. Tranexemic acid
	6. Inejection of the greater palatine canal
