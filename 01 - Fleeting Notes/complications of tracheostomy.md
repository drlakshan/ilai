# What Did I learn?


Overall : 3.2%
Mortality : 06%
Higher risk when : airway infections, obesity, paralysis, CHF


Intraprocedural
	airway fire (rare, reduce FiO2, stop cautery at entry)
	Bleeding
	Pneumothorax/pneumomediatinum
	Paratracheal insertion
	Posterior wall laceration (especially percutenous)

Early <7 days
	Infection 6.6%
	Tube obstruction 
	Pressure ulcers (from faceplate, obesity kyphoscoliosis)
	Accidental decanulation
	Loss of airway

Late >7days
	tracheal stenois
	tracheo-innominate fistula low placement malnutrition radiation
	tracheo-cutaneous fistula higher risk with Bjork flap
	tracheomalacia
	delayed stoma closure
