###### sjogren's disease diagnosis
2022-12-22- 06:06





######  Note


1. Clinical features for more than three months 
	1. dry eyes
	2. dry mouth
2. Bloods
	1. ANA + >1:320 80%
	2. Rheumatoid factor + 70 to 90%
	3. anti Ro >60% and anti La 40%
3. Salivary flow rate [[reduced salivary flow rate in Sjogrens]]
4. Tear flow reduction [[Shirmer's test]]
5. [[Minor Salivary gland biopsy]]
