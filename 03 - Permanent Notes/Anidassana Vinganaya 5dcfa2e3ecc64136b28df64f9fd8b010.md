# Anidassana Vinganaya

Created: December 17, 2020 6:24 AM
Importance: VERY HIGH

patawi aapo thejo vayo yana sathara maha bootha rupa naththe koheda yana prashnayata pilithuru soyamin giya ekthara bhikshuwak gana kiywena kathawak

Suthraya : Kewadda Suthraya

Mehidi Kewadda nam gruahpathoyata Budu Piyanan wahanse me abingha laabee bikshuwa gana kiyadenawea. Me bhikshuewa me sathra maha bhoohta rupa nathi thanak soyamin yana athere brahmayen gen wimasanawa ethakota e brahmayan pawasanawa Budu Piyanan gen ma asannata kiya.

Ethokata e prashnaya yam sakaskireemak karanawa. E prashnaya wardai. Nathi thanak neme. no pihitana thanak thama asiya yuththe. 

Anidassana vingnganaya yanu eyay. Abhissankatha vinganaya yanu naama rupa dharmayangen sakaswanna wu

Anidassana V yanu appatiththita V

Abisankatha V yanu patiththita V
