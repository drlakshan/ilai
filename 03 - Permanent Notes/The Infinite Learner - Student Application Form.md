## Application Instructions

Thank you for your interest in The Infinite Learner program. This pilot cohort will help shape the future of medical education by combining evidence-based learning science, AI tools, and mindfulness practices.

**Pilot Program Benefits:**
- Free course access (valued at $400-500)
- Certificate of completion from Dr. MTD Lakshan
- Early access to AI learning tools
- Contribution to medical education research
- Opportunity to become program ambassador

- **Basic Course:** 8 weeks, 4-6 hours/week
- **Advanced Course:** 10 weeks, 6-8 hours/week

**Selection Criteria:**
- Genuine motivation to improve learning skills
- Commitment to providing detailed feedback
