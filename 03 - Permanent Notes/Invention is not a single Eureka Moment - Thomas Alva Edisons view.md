- **Invention often involves a robust understanding of business and marketing strategies.** The sources highlight that Edison's success as an inventor was not solely due to his technical prowess. Edison was also a shrewd businessman who understood the importance of marketing his inventions effectively. By understanding the power of public image and skillfully navigating the market, Edison ensured that his inventions reached a wide audience and achieved lasting commercial success.
