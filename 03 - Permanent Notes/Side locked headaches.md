###### Side locked headaches
2022-06-23- 15:18


######  Note

a group of headaches that characteristically occurring on one side of the head.

e.g. 
1. [[Cluster Headaches Definition]]
2. [[paroxysmal hemicrania]]
3. hemicrania continua
4. short lasting neuralgic type
