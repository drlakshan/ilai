###### two types of breaks in humane productivity system
2022-07-24- 12:39





######  Note


1. 15 minute blank breaks - destimulating - doing nothing
2. replenishing breaks : hobbies, movies, etc






Context: It is important to take breaks from work however when we take breaks we tend to overstimulate but in the blank breaks it should be destimulating
