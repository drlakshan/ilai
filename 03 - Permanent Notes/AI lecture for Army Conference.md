• **Imaging Analysis:**

▪ Identifying patterns in patient symptoms and historical data for predictive analytics.

• **Robotic Surgery:**

▪ Integration of AI in improving precision during ENT surgeries.

• **Decision Support:**

• **Training & Simulation:**

• **Simulation Platforms:**

▪ How AI-powered simulations are being used to train military medical personnel.

• **Virtual Reality (VR):**

▪ Immersive environments for practicing surgical techniques and diagnosis.
