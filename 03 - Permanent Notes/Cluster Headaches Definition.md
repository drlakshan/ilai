###### Cluster Headaches Definition
2022-06-22- 10:18


######  Note

very severe
unilateral headache lasting 15 to 180 minutes
up to 8 times a day
autonomic features  conjunctival injection, lacrimation, nasal congestion, rhinorrhoea, eye lid oedema
sense of agitation / restlessness
