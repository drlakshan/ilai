MMA
Enter Bulla and remove superior medial and inferior walls
then open up the rest of the anterior ethmoidal cells

then can proceed to sphenoid sinus and do a retrograde dissection of the anterior skull base

Another approach is perforation at the medial and inferior wall of the basal lamella at the height of the roof of the maxillary sinus and enter the posterior ethmoidal complex.
