## 2. Purpose & Objectives

This Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) establishes a collaborative partnership to advance medical education through evidence-based learning strategies and AI integration. The partnership aims to:

1. **Provide educational opportunities** to medical students and healthcare professionals
2. **Promote innovative learning methodologies** combining learning science, AI tools, and Buddhist mindfulness
3. **Conduct educational research** on effective learning in the AI era
4. **Foster institutional collaboration** in medical education excellence

## 5. Financial Arrangements

### 5.1 Student Participation Model

**Option A: Subsidized Access**
- Partner institution negotiates group rate: [Amount] per student
- Institution invoiced quarterly based on enrollment
- Payment terms: Net 30 days

**Option B: Institutional License**
- Annual license fee: [Amount] for unlimited students
- Includes customization for institutional branding
- Payment terms: 50% upfront, 50% at 6 months

**Option C: Free Pilot Phase**
- First cohort (up to [Number] students) offered at no cost
- Institution commits to data collection and feedback
- Future cohorts at negotiated rate

**Option D: Individual Student Payment**
- Students pay directly to program
- Partner institution provides promotional support only
- No financial obligation to institution

### 5.2 Resource Sharing
- No exchange of funds for promotional activities
- Each party bears own operational costs
- Shared resources (if any) documented in addendum

## 6. Intellectual Property

**Program Content:**
- The Infinite Learner retains all IP rights to curriculum, materials, and tools
- Partner institution may use program materials only for promotional purposes

**Joint Development:**
- Any co-created content ownership negotiated separately
- Attribution to both parties for collaborative work

**Student Work:**
- Students retain rights to their original work
- Program may use anonymized work for educational purposes with consent

## 7. Data Sharing & Research

**Data Collection:**
- Student enrollment data (names, emails, cohort)
- Academic performance data (assessment scores, completion)
- Feedback surveys and qualitative responses

**Data Use:**
- Program improvement and curriculum refinement
- Educational research and publication
- Aggregate reporting to stakeholders

**Data Protection:**
- All personal data anonymized for research
- Compliance with institutional IRB/ethics requirements
- Secure storage with access controls

**Publication Rights:**

- Attribution to partner institution in acknowledgments

## 10. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to:
- Maintain confidentiality of proprietary information
- Not disclose sensitive institutional data
- Protect student privacy per applicable laws
- Use information only for purposes of this agreement

**Exceptions:**
- Information already in public domain
- Disclosure required by law
- Information independently developed

## 11. Termination

**Termination by Notice:**
- Either party may terminate with 90 days written notice
- Current enrolled students complete program

**Termination for Cause:**
- Immediate termination for material breach
- 30 days to remedy breach before termination
- Examples: data breach, quality failure, non-payment

**Post-Termination:**
- Student data returned or destroyed per agreement
- Outstanding financial obligations settled
- Marketing materials with partner branding removed

## 13. Miscellaneous Provisions

- Amendments attached as addenda

**Entire Agreement:**
- This MOU constitutes entire understanding
- Supersedes prior negotiations or agreements

**No Partnership:**
- This MOU does not create legal partnership or joint venture
- Each party remains independent entity
