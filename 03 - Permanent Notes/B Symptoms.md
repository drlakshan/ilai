###### B Symptoms
2022-07-03- 21:40





######  Note


In lymphadenopathy patients presence of B symptoms may signify haematolgical malignacy


1. unexplained fever >38 Celsius
2. unintentional weight loss >10% usual body weight within past 6 months
3. drenching night sweats necessitating change of bed clothes





not pruritus, alcohol intolerance,
