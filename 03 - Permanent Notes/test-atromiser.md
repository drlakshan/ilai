The use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in medical education holds the potential to facilitate complicated tasks and improve overall efficiency. See also: [[AI in Healthcare]], [[Educational Technology]], [[Workflow Automation]]

AI can be utilized to automate the assessment of written responses in a medical education context, saving time for educators and providing scalable feedback. See also: [[Natural Language Processing in Education]], [[Automated Essay Scoring]], [[Assessment Methods]]

AI offers the capability to provide feedback on medical image interpretations with a high degree of reliability, which can be a powerful tool for training in fields like radiology and pathology. See also: [[Computer-Aided Diagnosis]], [[Medical Imaging]], [[Feedback Mechanisms in Learning]]

While the applications of AI in learning, instruction, and assessment are currently growing, the field still requires further exploration to understand its full potential and limitations. See also: [[Future of Medical Education]], [[Research Gaps in AI]], [[AI Adoption in Academia]]

There is a notable lack of conceptual or methodological guides designed for medical educators who wish to either evaluate or actively engage in AI-related research. See also: [[Medical Education Research]], [[AI Research Methodology]], [[Guides for Educators]]

This AMEE guide aims to describe the practical considerations involved in both reading and conducting studies that use AI within the domain of medical education. See also: [[Conducting AI Research]], [[Literature Review in AI]], [[AMEE Guides]]

The guide also serves to define basic terminology related to Artificial Intelligence, making the field more accessible to medical educators. See also: [[AI Terminology]], [[Glossary of Machine Learning]], [[Foundational Concepts of AI]]

A key objective of the guide is to identify which specific problems and data types within medical education are ideally-suited for the application of AI solutions. See also: [[AI Use Cases in Medical Education]], [[Data Suitability for AI]], [[Problem-Based Learning]]
