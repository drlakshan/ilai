# Refluxate

#physiology #stub

Refluxate is the material that flows back from the stomach. In the context of [[Pathophysiology of Laryngopharyngeal Reflux]], it is a complex mixture containing hydrochloric acid, enzymes like [[Pepsin]], bile acids, and bacteria.

In LPR, it is often delivered as a gaseous aerosol rather than liquid.
