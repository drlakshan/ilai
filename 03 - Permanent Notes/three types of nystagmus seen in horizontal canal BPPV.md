Three types of nystagmus associated with  [[Horizontal canal BPPV]]

1. bilateral geotrophic : particles in the posterior part of the HZ canal
2. bilateral apogeotrophic: particles in the anterior part of the HZ canal
3. bilateral apogeotrophic that never changes charachteristics cupulolithiasis
([Casani et al 2002:173](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/DMMBP7RE?page=2))

Sometimes this third type - cupulolithaisis type - will be confused with a cenrtal cause CV Junction Up-beating nystagmus ([note on p.173](zotero://open-pdf/library/items/DMMBP7RE?page=2))
