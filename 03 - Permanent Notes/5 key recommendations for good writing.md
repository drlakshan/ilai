5 Key recommendations for quality writing according to Editors Victor Dwyer and Charlotte Gill

1. *Prioritize editing and revision*: Embrace editing as an essential process to refine your writing and improve overall quality. Cultivate a habit of revising and drafting extensively to ensure clarity and engagement in your work.

2. *Develop a unique voice*: Focus on creating an authentic writing style that connects deeply with readers. This will help you stand out in the crowded media landscape and build a loyal audience.

3. *Seek feedback and mentorship*: Engage with experienced writers for guidance and constructive criticism to enhance your craft and storytelling skills.

4. *Balance creativity and commercial viability:* Learn to navigate the changing media landscape by finding a balance between creative expression and meeting market demands.

5. *Diversify your reading and inspiration* Regularly read across genres and engage with various sources of inspiration to inform and enrich your writing projects, leading to more diverse and compelling storytelling techniques.
