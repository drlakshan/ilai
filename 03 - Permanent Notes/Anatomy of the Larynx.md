# Anatomy of the Larynx

#anatomy #stub

Understanding the anatomy of the larynx is a prerequisite for understanding the [[Pathophysiology of Laryngopharyngeal Reflux]].

The larynx is lined with delicate respiratory and squamous epithelium, which is far less resilient to damage from [[Refluxate]] and [[Pepsin]] than the oesophagus.
