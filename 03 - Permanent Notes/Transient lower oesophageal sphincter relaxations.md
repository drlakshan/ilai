# Transient lower oesophageal sphincter relaxations

#physiology #stub

Transient lower oesophageal sphincter relaxations (TLESRs) are a primary mechanism that allows for the backflow of gastric [[Refluxate]] into the oesophagus and potentially the larynx.

This is a key concept in the [[Pathophysiology of Laryngopharyngeal Reflux]].
