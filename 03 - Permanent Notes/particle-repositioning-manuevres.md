## <font color=7FFFD4>Posterior Canal Protocols</font>
- **[[Epley maneuver]]** – standard clinic liberatory manoeuvre for posterior-canal BPPV; reinforce [[Post Epley instructions]] afterwards.
- **[[Semont maneuver]]** – rapid liberatory alternative; see [[Semont Plus Maneuvre]] for extended variant.
- **[[Brandt -Daroff exercises]]** – home habituation sequence when symptoms persist or recur; contrasting features captured in [[Difference between Semont and Brandt Darroff manoeuvres]].


## <font color=7FFFD4>Horizontal Canal Protocols</font>
- **[[barbacue rotation]] / log roll** – sequential 90° rotations; success rates summarised in [[Success rate of HZ canal positioning manoeuvre]].
- **[[Modified Sermont - Gafouni ]]** – “Gufoni” manoeuvre especially for apogeotropic horizontal canal BPPV; outlined within [[Horizontal canal BPPV]].
- **[[Forced Prolonged Position]]** – patient maintains side-lying posture overnight; based on Vannucchi et al. (1997) and subsequent [[Treatment of Horizontal Canal Nystagmus - Casani et al 2002]].


## <font color=7FFFD4>When to Suspect Non-BPPV Causes</font>
Refer to [[Central Positional Vertigo]] and [[Atypical BPPV - criteria to be careful about]] when manoeuvres fail, nystagmus is atypical, or neurological signs accompany vertigo. These notes outline red flags, imaging triggers and counselling points.
