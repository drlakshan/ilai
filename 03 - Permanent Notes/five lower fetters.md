# References

Here are the Pali terms for each of the ten fetters (samyojana) in Buddhism:

1. Sakkāya-diṭṭhi - Self-identity views
2. Vicikicchā - Doubt or uncertainty
3. Sīlabbata-parāmāsa - Attachment to rites and rituals
4. Kāma-rāga - Sensual desire
5. Vyāpāda - Ill will

These are the five lower fetters (orambhāgiya-saṃyojana). The five higher fetters (uddhambhāgiya-saṃyojana) are:

6. Rūpa-rāga - Passion for form or material existence
7. Arūpa-rāga - Passion for formless or immaterial existence
8. Māna - Conceit
9. Uddhacca - Restlessness
10. Avijjā - Ignorance
