## Role 1: Program Director

**Role Holder:** Dr. MTD Lakshan

### Core Responsibilities
- Overall program vision and strategic direction

- Partnership cultivation and stakeholder management
- Public representation and thought leadership
- Course facilitation (weekly sessions)
- Final decision-making authority

### Time Commitment
- **Phase 0-2:** 0.3 FTE (12 hours/week)
- **Phase 3-5:** 0.4 FTE (16 hours/week)
- **Phase 6+:** 0.4 FTE (16 hours/week)

### Key Activities
**Weekly:**
- Facilitate 1-2 live course sessions (90 min each)
- Review curriculum development progress (2 hours)

- Student feedback review and response (1 hour)
- Team coordination meetings (1 hour)

**Monthly:**
- Strategic planning and program evolution (4 hours)
- Content creation (lectures, case studies) (8 hours)

- Marketing content (LinkedIn posts, interviews) (2 hours)

**Quarterly:**
- Comprehensive program review (8 hours)

### Success Metrics
- Student satisfaction scores (NPS >50)
- Enrollment targets met (80% capacity)
- Partnership retention rate (>90%)
- Program completion rates (>75%)
- Personal brand growth (LinkedIn followers, speaking invitations)

### Compensation
- **Phase 0-2:** Deferred or equity stake

✓ Medical expertise (ENT specialty)

✓ Strategic thinking
✓ Stakeholder management

- Monthly literature reviews on AI and education
- Recommend curriculum updates based on emerging evidence
- Validate educational approaches with research
- Support research publication efforts

### Time Commitment

- **Phase 3-5:** 0.3 FTE (12 hours/week)
- **Phase 6+:** 0.2 FTE (8 hours/week)

### Key Activities
**Weekly:**
- Literature monitoring (journals, research databases) (3 hours)
- Curriculum review and evidence validation (2 hours)
- Team consultation on educational strategies (1 hour)

**Monthly:**
- Comprehensive literature review report (6 hours)
- Curriculum recommendations memo (4 hours)
- Research on AI tools in education (4 hours)

**Quarterly:**
- Major curriculum revision based on new evidence (12 hours)
- Research paper draft (if publishing program outcomes) (8 hours)

### Deliverables
- Monthly literature review summaries
- Evidence-based curriculum recommendations
- Assessment validity reviews
- Research publication support

### Success Metrics
- Curriculum alignment with latest research (100%)
- Number of evidence-based improvements implemented (>10/year)
- Research publications co-authored (target 1-2/year)
- Student learning outcome improvements attributed to evidence-based changes

### Compensation
- **Phase 0-2:** $4,000-6,000 (4 months)
- **Phase 3+:** $2,000-3,000/month retainer

### Required Skills
✓ PhD in Education, Cognitive Psychology, or related field (preferred)
✓ Medical education background
✓ Research methodology expertise
✓ Literature review and synthesis
✓ Statistical analysis
✓ Academic writing

- Education researcher with interest in AI and medical education
- Retired medical educator with research background

## Role 3: Educational Content Architect

**Role Type:** Part-time team member or Dr. Lakshan + AI

### Core Responsibilities
- Design course structure and learning modules
- Create educational content (presentations, readings, activities)
- Develop assessment frameworks
- Produce video lectures and visual materials
- Convert Dr. Lakshan's expertise into teachable content
- Maintain content library and course materials

### Time Commitment
- **Phase 0-2:** 0.5 FTE (20 hours/week) - high content creation phase
- **Phase 3-5:** 0.3 FTE (12 hours/week) - refinement phase
- **Phase 6+:** 0.2 FTE (8 hours/week) - maintenance and updates

### Key Activities
**Phase 0-2 (Development):**
- Structure 8-10 weeks of curriculum per course (30 hours)
- Create 20-30 video lecture scripts (40 hours)
- Develop practice activities and assignments (20 hours)
- Design visual aids and presentations (30 hours)
- Write student-facing materials (guides, FAQs) (20 hours)

**Phase 3-5 (Refinement):**
- Weekly content updates based on feedback (3 hours/week)
- Guest speaker coordination and briefing (2 hours/week)
- Assessment creation and grading support (4 hours/week)

**Phase 6+ (Maintenance):**
- Monthly content refresh (6 hours/month)
- New module development as needed (variable)
- Seasonal course updates (quarterly, 8 hours)

### Deliverables
- Complete course syllabi (Basic + Advanced)
- 30-50 video lectures (15-20 min each)
- 20-30 practice activities with solutions
- Assessment bank (100+ questions)
- Student guides and resource materials
- Visual presentation library

### Success Metrics
- Content completion on schedule
- Student engagement with materials (>80% video completion)
- Content clarity ratings (>4/5)
- Minimal clarification questions (indicates clear content)

### Compensation
- **Phase 0-2:** $3,000-5,000 (4 months)
- **Phase 3-5:** $1,500-2,500 (5 months)
- **Phase 6+:** $1,000-2,000/month

### Required Skills
✓ Instructional design
✓ Content creation (video, presentations, written)
✓ Medical knowledge or ability to learn quickly
✓ Proficiency with AI tools (Claude, ChatGPT for content generation)
✓ Graphic design basics
✓ Learning management system familiarity

### AI-Enhanced Approach
- Use Claude Skills for initial content drafts
- Dr. Lakshan reviews and adds clinical expertise
- Content architect polishes and produces final materials
- **Time savings:** 40-60% through AI collaboration

## Team Culture & Values

### Core Values
1. **Evidence-Based:** All decisions grounded in research and data
2. **Student-Centric:** Every action prioritizes learner outcomes
3. **Collaborative:** Cross-functional teamwork and mutual support
4. **Innovative:** Embrace AI and new methodologies thoughtfully
5. **Mindful:** Buddhist principles of compassion and presence
6. **Transparent:** Open communication and honest feedback

### Communication Norms
- **Weekly Team Sync:** 60 min, all hands (virtual)
- **Async Updates:** Daily Slack/email check-ins
- **Monthly All-Hands:** Strategic review and celebration
- **Quarterly Retreats:** Deep work and team building (optional)

### Decision-Making Framework (RACI)
- **Program Director:** Final authority on vision, quality, partnerships
- **Team Leads:** Authority within domain (tech, marketing, curriculum)
- **Consensus:** Major program changes discussed collectively
- **Rapid:** Operational decisions made quickly by relevant role

### Performance Management
- Monthly 1:1s with Program Director
- Quarterly goal setting and review (OKRs)
- Annual comprehensive performance evaluation
- Continuous feedback culture (no surprises)
