# acyclovir


Drug-Category :
anti virals


Brand Names :
zorivax
herperax-800

Doses:
400mg
800mb

Indications:
[[herpes zoster]]
herpes labialis
herpes genitalis
viral encephalitis




Side Effects :


DON'T use in :
renal failure as it is excreted in urine 
can cause acyclovir induced nephropathy ask patient to hydrate and maintain good urine output

hypersensitivity
milk protein hypersensitivity
