## Research Areas

### Specialized ENT Procedures
- **Endoscopic Ear Surgery**: Pioneering work in endoscopic approaches for various otologic procedures including myringoplasty, mastoid exploration, stapedectomy, and glomus tympanicum excision
- **Endoscopic Assisted Transoral Approach (EATA)**: Minimally invasive surgery for parapharyngeal space tumors
- **Dacryocystorhinostomy (DCR)**: Endoscopic approaches to lacrimal drainage surgery
- **Piriform Sinus Fistula**: Minimally invasive endoscopic management of adult-onset piriform sinus fistulas

### Otology and Neurotology
- **Sudden Sensorineural Hearing Loss**: Evidence-based management protocols and treatment strategies
- **Tinnitus Management**: Cognitive Behavioral Therapy approaches in tinnitus treatment
- **Vestibular Disorders**: Vestibular migraine diagnosis and management, vertigo syndromes prevalence and treatment efficacy
- **Cochlear Implants**: Comparative studies on cochleostomy vs. round window approaches
- **Gradenigo Syndrome**: Case studies and management approaches for rare complications of otitis media

### Head and Neck Surgery
- **Juvenile Nasal Angiofibroma**: Surgical approach optimization and resection techniques
- **Head and Neck Cancer**: Virtual multidisciplinary team (MDT) approaches using telemedicine
- **Laryngo-tracheal Stenosis**: Surgical management and outcomes

### COVID-19 Research
- **Telemedicine**: Application of telemedicine in otorhinolaryngology during the pandemic
- **Infection Prevention**: Povidone iodine as nasal and oral antiseptic for healthcare worker protection
- **Procedure-Related Complications**: Epistaxis following COVID-19 nasopharyngeal swabbing

### Sensory Disorders
- **Anosmia Management**: Holistic treatment approaches including smell retraining therapy and cognitive behavioral therapy

### Rare Conditions
- **Conidiobolomycosis**: Rhinofacial infection case series and literature review

### Medical Education and Technology
- **Artificial Intelligence**: Integration of AI and ChatGPT in medical education
- **Digital Health Solutions**: Software development for medical portfolio management

### Surgical Techniques and Outcomes
- **Suture Techniques**: Comparative studies on wound closure methods in middle ear surgery
- **Consent and Ethics**: Studies on informed consent patterns and breaking bad news in cancer patients
- **Clinical Documentation**: Analyses of prescription writing patterns and surgical audit

### Healthcare Systems and Quality Improvement
- **Clinical Audits**: Multicentre studies on clinical outcomes, theatre documentation, and training opportunities
- **Resource Utilization**: Studies on accuracy of electronic data and reimbursement systems
- **Referral Quality**: Assessment and improvement of referral letter quality in clinical practice
