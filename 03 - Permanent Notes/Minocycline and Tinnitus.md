## <font color=DEB887>Minocycline and Tinnitus</font> 
2022-04-15- 14:00


##  <font color=7FFFD4>Note</font> 

Minocyline can cause  [[Tinnitus]] on its own merit and it can induce Idiopathic Intracranial hypertension [[Idiopathic Intracranial Hypertension]] that can cause tinnitus as well.
