###### 5 different nerve pathways that innervate the ear
2022-09-12- 05:56





######  Note

CONTEXT:

when patient with ear pain and pressure comes to you, there are possibilities along these nerve pathways

1. Trigeminal : from oral cavity
2. Vagus : larynx and oesophagus
3. Glossopharyngeal : throat and tongue base
4. Facial : Teeth TMJ and Parotid
5. C2, C3 Spinal Nerve : Neck
