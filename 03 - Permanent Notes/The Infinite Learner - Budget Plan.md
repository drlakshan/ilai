## Cost Optimization Strategies

### Personnel Costs (Largest Expense)

**Reduce:**
1. Dr. Lakshan defers compensation initially (sweat equity)
2. Recruit medical students as teaching assistants (low cost/volunteer)
3. Engage retired medical educators (reduced rates, passion project)
4. Use Claude Skills extensively to reduce content creation time
5. Part-time contractors vs full-time staff

**Expected Savings:** 40-50% of personnel budget

### Technology Costs

**Reduce:**
1. Use open-source LMS (Moodle) instead of paid platforms
2. Leverage free tiers of AWS (12 months)
3. Use Claude API efficiently (caching, prompt optimization)
4. Build MVP tools (basic functionality first)
5. Use free video conferencing (Google Meet, Zoom free tier)

**Expected Savings:** 60-70% of technology budget

### Marketing Costs

**Reduce:**
1. Organic social media (no paid ads initially)
2. Partner institution channels (free promotion)
3. Word-of-mouth from pilot participants
4. Content marketing (blog, LinkedIn posts) vs paid advertising
5. Dr. Lakshan's existing network for initial outreach

**Expected Savings:** 70-80% of marketing budget

## Financial Assumptions

**Conservative Revenue Assumptions:**
1. Pilot cohort (50 students) converts at 60% to paying Year 1
2. Individual pricing: $300 (Basic), $400 (Advanced)
3. Only 1 institutional license sold Year 1
4. No major grants secured (purely operational revenue)

**Optimistic Revenue Scenarios:**

- 3 institutional licenses: +$15,000 - $25,000
- Higher enrollment (150 students): +$15,000

**Cost Assumptions:**

2. Team works at reduced rates or volunteer initially
3. Maximum use of free/open-source tools

## Budget Control Mechanisms

### Monthly Financial Review
- Track actuals vs budget
- Identify variances >10%
- Adjust spending in real-time

### Approval Thresholds
- <$500: Project Lead approval
- $500-$2,000: Team consensus
- >$2,000: Stakeholder review (if partners involved)

### Contingency Reserve
- Maintain 10% of total budget as buffer
- Release only for critical unforeseen needs

### Financial Reporting
- Monthly budget reports to stakeholders
- Quarterly financial health assessment
- Annual comprehensive financial review
