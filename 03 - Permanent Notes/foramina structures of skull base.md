

*   **Optic canal**:  The optic canal transmits the **optic nerve (CN II)** and the **ophthalmic artery**.

*   **Superior orbital fissure**: The superior orbital fissure transmits the **lacrimal nerve**, **frontal nerve**, **trochlear nerve (CN IV)**, **superior ophthalmic vein**, **nasociliary nerve**, **inferior ophthalmic vein**, **abducens nerve (CN VI)**, **oculomotor nerve (CN III)**, and **ophthalmic nerve (CN V1)**.

*   **Foramen ovale**: The foramen ovale transmits the **mandibular nerve (CN V3)**.
