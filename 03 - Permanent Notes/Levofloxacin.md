# Levofloxacin


Category :

Quinalone

Brand Names :
Leflox

Doses:

500mg a day

Indications:
Otitis externa


Side Effects :
Tendon rupture
Neuromuscular transmission block therefore avoid in myasthenia gravis
Prolonged QT interval

DON'T use in :
Cartilage issues
