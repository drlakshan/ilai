# Amitriptyline


Category :

Tricyclic antidepressant
decrease in re-uptake of norepinephrine and seratonin

Brand Names :

Tryptanol
Elavil

Doses:
5mg
10mg
12.5mg
25mg


Indications:

1. vestibular migraine
2. migraine
3. mid-facial pain
4. globus
5. TMJ syndrome
6. neuropathic pain

Side Effects :

1. drowsiness
2. weight gain
3. dry mouth



DON'T use in :
MI
MOAI therapy
CBMZPine hypersensitivity
