**Calculation**: The hurdle rate is generally calculated by adding a risk premium to the weighted average cost of capital (WACC). The risk premium reflects the additional return required to compensate for the risk of the investment. The formula is:  
Hurdle Rate=WACC+Risk PremiumHurdle Rate=WACC+Risk Premium  
This calculation can also consider factors like inflation and interest rates
