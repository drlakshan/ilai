# Venlafaxine


Category : SNRI
serotonin and norepinephrine reuptake inhibitors

active metabolite  :O-dv (O - deoxymethyl venlafaxine)


Brand Names :
venix

Doses: 37.mg BD

can do 37.5mg daily for a week and then increase to 37.5mg BD 
outpatient max 225mg


Indications: PPPD Migraine Prophylaxis [[Vestibular migraine Definition]]
Panic Disorder
Anxiety


Side Effects : 
BLOOD PRESSURE and HEART RATE INCREASE
Sertonin reuptake in platelets also reduced therefore bleeding risk


DONT use in : 

SEVERE HIGH BP
