Meditated at the Kirivehera Stupa. It was a very pleasing experience. Bestowed merits to Kataragama Deviyo for all the help and protection given to me. I wished Him well and wanted him to be happy and more properous. 
At the same time Maha Brahma, Bodhisathva, other powerful and less powerful, known and unknown deities were commemorated and merits bestowed upon.
All living and dead relatives were commemorated and mertis bestowed upon.
Donated to devalaya and helpful person.Saiya

It was all giving not begging or pleading. So powerful
