# Meditation progression

Date: February 3, 2021
Signicant Event: No

Just as a gem becomes prettier and clearer when you observe from many angles, Dhamma becomes clearer and better understood when you look at it many different angles. However the visual angle - දෘෂ්ටි කෝණය is very important.
