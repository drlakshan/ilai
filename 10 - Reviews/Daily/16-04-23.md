Being a Buddhist I have always tried to think in a broader way about life. we have a framework that we think about life. Take for example:

# four principles of life:

1. Upaneeyathi loko addo : worldly existence is a thing that will slowly progress in to delipidated siuation whether we like it or not.
2. Aththano loko anabissaro : there is no protection in this worldly existence
3. Assako loko sabban pahaya gamaneeyan: we will have to leave everything held dear to us one day
4. Uno loko athitho thanha daso : the worldly existence is one which always leaves something more to be desired.
