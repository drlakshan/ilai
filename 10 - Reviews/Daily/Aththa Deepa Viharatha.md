# Aththa Deepa Viharatha

Date: October 3, 2022
Signicant Event: Yes

From the 1st of October I have dedicated to this concept. 

Remember Ikigai Concept?

Where what you are good at, what you like, what the people want and what is been paid for intersect. For this aspect using principle of Ikigai developement I will work but as the backbone of all of these there is this concept of self actualisation. 

This can be achieved only through buddhism.
