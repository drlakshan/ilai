Dr. M T D Lakshan

MBBS MS DOHNS(UK) FEB ORL-HNS (EUROPE) FRCSEd ORL-HNS

Consultant ENT & Head and Neck Surgeon

With over two decades of dedicated experience, Dr. MTD Lakshan is a highly distinguished Consultant ENT and Head and Neck Surgeon. Holding prestigious qualifications including MBBS (Col), MS(Oto), DOHNS(Eng), FEB ORL-HNS (Europe), and FRCSEd ORL-HNS, he is committed to providing exceptional, patient-centered care.

As a Director at Nawaloka Hospitals PLC and Head of the Nawaloka Hospitals Research and Education Foundation, Dr. Lakshan is at the forefront of medical innovation and education in Sri Lanka. His expertise spans a comprehensive range of ear, nose, and throat conditions, ensuring the highest standards of treatment and surgical excellence.
